  • Background

I'm a graduate student pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at University of North Texas. I currently work as a Research Assistant in the Information Retrieval and Text Mining Lab supervised by Dr. Wei Jin. Before, I have been part of the Machine Learning Research Lab supervised by Dr. Cornelia Caragea.
I completed my M.S. in Computer Science and B.S. in Mathematics and Computer Science at University of Bucharest, Romania.




  • Research Interest

I am interested in exploring fields like machine learning, natural language processing, text mining, and artificial intelligence. Recently, I've focused my research efforts on summarization with applications on keyphrase extraction.
